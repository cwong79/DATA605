  1. Given an mxn matrix where m > n, what can be the maximum rank? The mini- mum rank, assuming that the matrix is non-zero?

For a rectangular m × n matrix, the largest possible rank is the smaller of the two dimensions. In the case where m > n, the largest possible rank is n.

A non-zero matrix has a minimum of one entry, meaning it has a minimum of one pivot. As the rank is equivalent to the number of pivots, the minimum possible rank is 1.
